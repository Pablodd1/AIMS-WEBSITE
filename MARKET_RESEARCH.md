## 📊 Market Insights

### Key Pain Points (Physician Perspective)
1. **Documentation Burden:** 50% of workday spent on admin tasks
2. **Burnout:** 63% of physicians report burnout symptoms
3. **After-hours Charting:** 1-3 hours daily spent on documentation
4. **Revenue Loss:** Missed billing codes = $50K-150K annually per provider
5. **Patient Care Time:** Only 27% of time spent with patients

### ROI Metrics That Resonate
- **Time Savings:** 15+ minutes per patient visit
- **Revenue Increase:** $127K average annual savings per physician
- **Patient Volume:** +4 patients daily capacity
- **ROI Timeline:** 3-12 months payback period
- **Accuracy:** 95-99% documentation accuracy

### Buying Decision Factors
1. **EHR Integration:** Must work with Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth
2. **Accuracy:** 95%+ accuracy rate essential
3. **HIPAA Compliance:** Non-negotiable
4. **Implementation Time:** Prefer <2 weeks setup
5. **Training Required:** Minimal disruption preferred
6. **Price:** $200-600/provider/month sweet spot
7. **Support:** 24/7 support highly valued
8. **Specialty-Specific:** Customization for medical specialty
